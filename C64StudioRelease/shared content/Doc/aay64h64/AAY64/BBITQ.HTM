BITQ            BIT Test bits in memory with Q register              BITQ

  Operation:  Q /\ M -> Z, M30 -> V, M31 -> N               N V - B D I Z C
                                                            / / . . . . / .
  Bit 30 and 31 are transferred to the status register.
  If the result of Q /\ M is zero then Z = 1, otherwise Z=0.
